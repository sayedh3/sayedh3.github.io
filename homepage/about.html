About the Ozone Park Pantry

The Ozone Park Pantry is committed to supporting families in the community and beyond. Since March 2020, the pantry has been serving families in need throughout City Line, Ozone Park, Woodhaven, South Ozone Park, and beyond. The Ozone Park Pantry is a weekly service that began at the height of the pandemic, serving over 1,000 families. Every Saturday at Digby Place and Rockaway Blvd, we distributed a large variety of fresh produce, dry goods, and canned goods. The pantry is open to anyone in need, judgment-free.
